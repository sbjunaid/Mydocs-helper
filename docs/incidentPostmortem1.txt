Incident Date: 2024-07-03  
Title: S3 Bucket Misconfiguration Exposed Logs  
Summary: Logs were accidentally made public due to incorrect IAM policy on an S3 bucket.  
Impact: 13 hours of public exposure; no evidence of data download.  
Root Cause: Dev environment bucket was promoted to prod without policy update.  
Resolution: Replaced bucket with restricted access and rotated logs.  
Next Steps:  
- Add policy checks in CI  
- Review all critical buckets weekly
